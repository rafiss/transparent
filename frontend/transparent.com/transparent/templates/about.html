Transparent.About


Have you ever used an online search engine to look for the cheapest products on the market? Have you ever found lower prices at retailers not included in those searches? Transparent is an open source effort to deliver the best deals on online retail stores to its users. It accomplishes this by opening up the development process to the community to expand the search to previously unreachable vendors. Transparent offers many features in including to the user.



Search Functionality

Transparent provides a very intuitive and simple interface to search for products, using the conventional search bar on the home page. Transparent is powered by Sphinx to deliver the most relevant search results to the user.


Settings

By signing up for a free account, you can customize the search results and tailor them specifically to your needs. This gives you the flexibility to choose whichever retailers or specific modules to use. The Settings tab also compactly shows all the modules along with their date of submission and the number of upvotes and downvotes the module received from other users. This provides additional information to help choose the most beneficial modules for a better searching experience.


Tracker

Another great feature that registered users can utilize is the Price Tracker. If you find a particular item that you want to purchase after it drops below a certain price, you can have this product tracked and displayed on the Tracked Items page. This provides a simple way to have all items of interest in one location, instead of having to search many websites to find the cheapest price over time.


Submit Module

You can help to improve the search experience by writing and submitting modules to Transparent using the Submit Module form. By following the Module API, you can make a module for your preferred retailer and have that included in the search results when making a query. Submitted modules are currently reviewed by the Transparent team, and are manually plugged into the backend.


Upvote/Downvote

This features allows users to upvote their favorite modules while downvoting modules that display incorrect or faulty information on the product page. This will help the better modules rise up and get used more, while unpopular modules can be removed from search results in the Settings page.


Price History

Ever wonder what the price trends are for the products you are interested in? With the price history feature, you can get an idea of what the cheapest price for a product has been over time, helping you make an informed decision for waiting or purchasing right away. Market trends are extremely useful for purchasing products at the right time, and Transparent brings this feature directly to you to help save you both time and money.
